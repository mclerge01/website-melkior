Guide

Move forward with the right reference points

A sound mortgage strategy starts before the application. Before choosing a rate or lender, it helps to understand your real budget, available cash, timing, and the flexibility you will need after the purchase. These reference points come up often in my conversations and make the decision easier to navigate.

Borrowing capacity

Knowing the maximum amount is useful, but choosing a comfortable payment matters even more.

Down payment

Each scenario has implications: mortgage insurance, remaining liquidity, startup costs, and flexibility.

Rate hold

A pre-approval may protect a rate while you shop, depending on lender conditions.
