Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

Website operator

This website is operated by Melkior Clergé, Multi-Prêts mortgage broker. For questions about this policy or your personal information, write to consultation@melkiorclerge.ca.

Information collected

The contact form collects the information needed to handle your request: name, email, phone, subject, referral source if provided, message, and request language. Cloudflare Turnstile may process technical information needed for anti-spam verification.

Use

This information is used to respond to your request, contact you about your mortgage project, and protect the form. No automated decision is made from this information.

Disclosure

Personal information is not sold. It is shared only with services needed to operate the website, host it, secure it, and send email, including Cloudflare. These services may process information outside Quebec or Canada.

Cookies

The website uses a language preference cookie to remember your choice between French and English. The administration section uses login cookies for authorized contributors only. The public website does not use advertising or analytics cookies.

Retention

The website does not keep a client database. Messages are retained in the professional email inbox for request follow-up, then deleted when no longer needed or according to applicable practices.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of submitted information, or withdraw consent where the law allows, by writing to consultation@melkiorclerge.ca.
